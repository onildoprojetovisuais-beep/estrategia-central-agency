## Core Methodology: Método Pediatra em Escala

The agency's entire offer is built around a 3-level value ladder:

| Level | Name       | Monthly Fee        | Goal                                              | Duration     |
|-------|------------|--------------------|---------------------------------------------------|--------------|
| N1    | Atração    | R$ 2.500/mês       | Fill agenda with qualified private patients       | 4 months     |
| N2    | Conversão  | R$ 3.750–5.000/mês | Convert 60–70% of leads into appointments         | 6–12 months  |
| N3    | Retenção   | R$ 5.000–7.000/mês | Maximize LTV through family retention             | 12–24+ months|

Average client LTV: **R$ 124.000 over 22 months**.

Offer model: 80% productized templates + 20% customization. Never pure custom work.

## Brand Voice Rules

### Nil (Onildo Figueiredo)
- Archetype: Creative with Magician traits
- Tone: Direct, human, no pretense; authority without arrogance; "pai" (father) perspective
- Forbidden: jargon, "barato/cheap", "expert", inflated promises
- CTA: one simple invitation per piece, no artificial urgency

### Jaque Souza
- Archetype: Strategist who lives what she analyzes
- Tone: Strategic, analytical, warm; sees details others miss; mother's perspective
- Forbidden: generic marketing terms, "solução", fragmented offers
- CTA: one direct invitation, clarity-focused, no artificial urgency

## Language and Positioning Rules

- Always use **internal language** (strategic, process-oriented) for documents and planning.
- Use **external/client language** (result-oriented, pain-aware) only in commercial copy and sales scripts.
- The pediatrician's core pain: empty schedule, tight cash flow. Lead with urgency of result; depth comes later.
- Never position as a generic medical marketing agency. The specialization in pediatrics IS the differentiator.
